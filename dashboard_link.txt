Note:
The dashboard reads live BigQuery mart tables through a dedicated read-only service account.

The demo was updated with a larger synthetic Greenhouse-like dataset to make the HR-facing dashboard more representative:
- 120 jobs
- 1,800 candidates
- 2,500 applications
- several thousand application stage events

The Hugging Face Space may take a few seconds to wake up on first load. If the page appears blank, please refresh once or wait briefly.
